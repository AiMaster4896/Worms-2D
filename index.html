GRUB WARS
a 2D destructible-terrain artillery skirmish
Two teams of three Grubs. Take turns lining up a shot, blow real holes into the terrain, and knock the other team into the sea.

Move: A/D or ←/→   Jump: W
Aim: ↑/↓   Charge & fire: hold then release Space
Switch weapon: 1 / 2   End turn early: Enter
Camera: drag to pan, scroll to zoom

Best played on desktop with a keyboard.
