GRUB WARS
a 2D destructible-terrain artillery skirmish
Two teams of three Grubs. Take turns lining up a shot, blow real holes into the terrain, and knock the other team into the sea.
On phone / tablet
Drag anywhere on the battlefield to aim — farther drag = more power. Release to fire.
Use the on-screen ◀ ⤒ ▶ buttons to move and jump. Pinch with two fingers to zoom.
On desktop
Move: A/D or ←/→   Jump: W
Aim: ↑/↓   Charge & fire: hold then release Space
Switch weapon: tap the weapon buttons or press 1 / 2

Rotate your phone sideways for the best view.
